## Task Management
Use `scripts/tasks.py` to manage tasks:
- `python3 scripts/tasks.py list`
- `python3 scripts/tasks.py create <category> <title>`
- `python3 scripts/tasks.py update <id> <status>`
- `python3 scripts/tasks.py show <id>`
